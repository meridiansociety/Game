SAFEHOUSE ASSAULT

Assault a fortified forest safehouse. Eliminate defenders, breach the building, and plant the bomb on the hardpoint. Once planted, defend it from enemy reinforcements until detonation.

Controls

WASD - Move
Mouse - Aim
Left Click - Fire
Shift - Sprint
C - Crouch
Space - Jump
R - Reload
1 / 2 - Switch weapon
E - Interact / Plant

Mission Rules

- Infiltrate the compound and reach the safehouse
- Plant the bomb at the hardpoint (hold E)
- Defend the bomb until detonation (~60s)
- You lose if killed, bomb defused, or 15min expires
- You win only when the bomb detonates while alive
- Manage ammo and health carefully
- Watch for enemy reinforcements after planting
