SAFEHOUSE ASSAULT

Assault a fortified forest safehouse, eliminate defenders, breach the house, and plant the bomb on the hardpoint. Once planted, hold the position and stop enemy reinforcements from defusing it.

Controls

WASD move
Mouse aim
Shift sprint
C crouch
R reload
1 / 2 switch weapons
E plant bomb / pick up item

Mission Rules

- You lose if health hits 0
- You lose if enemies defuse the bomb
- You lose when 15:00 expires
- You win only if the bomb detonates while you are alive
